# Natera (NTRA)

**Sub-sector:** Diagnostics (molecular testing - reproductive health, oncology MRD, organ health)
**One-liner:** The molecular-diagnostics scale leader, riding Signatera (oncology MRD)
from a reproductive-health base into the highest-growth, highest-value testing category.
**Profile last updated:** 2026-06-13

## Business model / how it makes money
Volume x ASP across three franchises:
- **Oncology (Signatera):** tumor-informed circulating-tumor-DNA (ctDNA) test for
  minimal/molecular residual disease (MRD) and recurrence monitoring - the growth engine
  and the most valuable category in diagnostics today.
- **Women's health (reproductive):** Panorama (NIPT prenatal screening), Horizon (carrier
  screening) - the original, cash-generative base.
- **Organ health (Prospera):** transplant-rejection monitoring via donor-derived ctDNA.
Revenue scales with test volume and ASP; payor coverage expansion (especially Signatera
indications) is the key driver.

## Competitive position
- **Guardant Health (Reveal), Tempus** - the direct MRD / liquid-biopsy competitors to Signatera.
- **Roche (Foundation Medicine), Illumina (Galleri)** - broader liquid-biopsy / genomics.
- **Myriad Genetics** - overlapping in hereditary / women's health.
Natera's tumor-informed Signatera approach and large coverage footprint are its moat in MRD.

## Why it matters for HC coverage
Natera is the bellwether for **MRD - the most strategically important growth category in
diagnostics**. At ~$30B it is the scaled, profitable-inflecting leader the whole liquid-biopsy
field is measured against, and a logical strategic target/acquirer in any consolidation thesis.
