# Tempus AI / Ambry Genetics (+ 2025 tuck-ins)

**Date:** Announced 2024-11-04; closed 2025-02-03
**Type:** Strategic M&A (acquisition of a private testing lab)
**Acquirer:** Tempus AI (TEM)
**Target:** Ambry Genetics (hereditary genetic testing; formerly owned by Konica Minolta)
**Sub-sector:** Diagnostics

## Terms
- **~$600M total:** ~$375M cash + ~$225M in Tempus shares at close ($100M of the stock
  subject to a one-year lock-up).
- Partly funded via a ~$300M financing raised ahead of close.

## Rationale
Adds a profitable, at-scale **hereditary genetic testing** franchise to Tempus, expanding
its diagnostics volume and germline menu alongside its oncology and data businesses.
Ambry drove the bulk of Tempus's FY2025 diagnostics growth (diagnostics revenue +~111% YoY,
much of it inorganic).

## Related Tempus 2025 acquisitions (same consolidation push)
- **Deep 6 AI** (2025-03): AI clinical-trial matching; added ~750 provider sites and ~30M
  patient records - feeds the Trials / Next applications.
- **Paige** (2025-08, ~$81.2M): AI cancer-pathology foundation models - strengthens the
  algorithmic/AI diagnostics stack.

## Valuation read
- Ambry at ~$600M was a reasonably-priced, profitable tuck-in relative to Tempus's own
  ~6-7x sales multiple - growth bought inorganically while the higher-margin data business
  compounds organically.

## Coverage angle
Tempus is the most acquisitive diagnostics name in the cycle - three deals in 2025. The
pattern (a scaled lab for volume + AI/data assets for differentiation) is the template for
how data-driven dx players build moats. Watch whether inorganic growth masks or accelerates
the organic data-business re-rate.
