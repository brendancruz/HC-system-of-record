# GeneDx (WGS)

**Sub-sector:** Diagnostics (exome / genome sequencing - rare disease & pediatrics)
**One-liner:** The rare-disease exome/genome leader executing one of the cleanest
turnarounds in diagnostics - shedding legacy testing to grow high-margin sequencing fast.
**Profile last updated:** 2026-06-13

## Business model / how it makes money
Volume x ASP, concentrated in **whole-exome and whole-genome sequencing** for rare and
hereditary disease, especially in pediatric / NICU settings, plus its Centrellis health-
intelligence data platform. GeneDx deliberately exited low-margin legacy product lines to
re-focus on exome/genome, which lifts both growth and gross margin. Revenue driven by test
volume, ASP, and expanding payor coverage / guideline inclusion for genome sequencing as a
first-line rare-disease test.

## Competitive position
- **Labcorp, Quest Diagnostics** - large reference labs with genetics menus.
- **Illumina** - sequencing platform / arm's-length enabler and occasional competitor.
- **Tempus, Natera** - adjacent in hereditary / genomic testing.
GeneDx's depth in rare-disease exome/genome interpretation (and its variant database) is
the differentiator vs. generalist labs.

## Recent deal / news (dated)
- **2025:** acquired **Fabric Genomics** (genomic interpretation / AI variant analysis) -
  bolsters the interpretation engine; modest near-term revenue contribution (~$3M-$5M post-close, est.).
- FY2025: ~$425M-$428M revenue with ~+50% exome/genome growth and ~74% adj. GM.

## Why it matters for HC coverage
GeneDx is the textbook diagnostics **turnaround/re-rate** - exit legacy, concentrate on
high-margin sequencing, watch growth and margin both inflect. A useful "cheaper growth"
counterweight to the richly valued MRD/screening names and a clean illustration of how
portfolio rationalization drives a re-rating.
