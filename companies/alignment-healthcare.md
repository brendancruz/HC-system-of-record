# Alignment Healthcare (ALHC)

**Sub-sector:** Payor-provider / managed care (Medicare Advantage insurtech)
**One-liner:** A tech-driven Medicare Advantage insurtech that is actually executing - high
growth plus improving medical loss ratio plus positive EBITDA, the bright spot in a battered MA cohort.
**Profile last updated:** 2026-06-14

## Business model / how it makes money
A consumer-focused **Medicare Advantage** plan operator built on a proprietary tech/data
platform (AVA) that targets care management for high-acuity seniors. Owns and operates MA plans
in CA, NC, NV (and manages care for some third-party MA HMOs). Profit = premium minus medical
cost (MBR) minus admin; the thesis is that better data-driven care management produces a lower,
improving MBR while membership scales fast - the opposite trajectory of the legacy MA players in 2025.

## Why it matters for HC coverage
Alignment is the counter-narrative to the 2025 MA crisis - proof that a disciplined, data-driven
MA model can grow fast *and* improve MBR while the incumbents struggle. The key "MA done right"
name and the most credible MA-insurtech scale story, which makes it both a momentum favorite and
a plausible strategic target.
