# Exact Sciences (EXAS - acquired by Abbott, March 2026)

**Sub-sector:** Diagnostics (cancer screening + precision oncology)
**One-liner:** The Cologuard colorectal-cancer screening franchise leader, now a
unit of Abbott after a ~$21B take-out that closed March 2026.
**Profile last updated:** 2026-06-13

## Business model / how it makes money
Volume x ASP diagnostics. Two reporting lines historically:
- **Screening** (the bulk): Cologuard, a non-invasive stool-DNA colorectal
  cancer screening test, plus the next-gen Cologuard Plus. Recurring, every-3-year
  re-screen dynamic with a large eligible US population. Reimbursed by Medicare and
  commercial payors; included in USPSTF / guideline recommendations.
- **Precision Oncology:** Oncotype DX (breast/prostate/colon tumor profiling) and
  newer blood-based assays (Oncodetect MRD, Cancerguard multi-cancer early detection
  in development).
Revenue scales with test volume and average selling price (ASP), so payor coverage
and guideline inclusion are the core value drivers.

## Valuation snapshot
- **No longer freely traded.** Abbott acquired Exact Sciences for **$105/share, ~$21B
  equity value**; agreement announced 2026-11-20 (2025), deal closed 2026-03-23.
- FMP price as of 2026-06-13 is pinned at **$104.91** (near the $105 deal price) with
  `isActivelyTrading = false`, consistent with the closed take-out.
- Implied take-out multiple: ~$21B / $3.25B FY2025 revenue = **~6.5x sales** - a
  premium-but-rational price for a profitable, scaled screening franchise (contrast
  GH/NTRA trading well above that on growth/optionality).

## Competitive position
Clear leader in stool-based CRC screening. The competitive frontier is moving to
**blood-based** screening, where it is challenged by:
- **Guardant Health (Shield)** - blood-based CRC screening, the most direct threat to
  Cologuard's franchise.
- **Grail (Galleri)** - multi-cancer early detection (MCED), competes with Cancerguard.
- **Natera** - overlapping in MRD / oncology with Oncodetect vs. Signatera.

## Recent deal / news (dated)

  Agreement first announced 2025-11-20. Now an Abbott diagnostics unit.

## Why it matters for HC coverage
This is *the* recent diagnostics mega-deal: a strategic ($21B, Abbott) validating that
a profitable, reimbursed screening franchise is worth ~6.5x sales while pre-profit
growth names trade double that. It reframes how to pitch diagnostics M&A - scaled +
profitable + recurring screening volume is the asset large-cap medtech will pay up for.
