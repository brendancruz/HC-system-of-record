# Innovaccer (Private)

**Sub-sector:** VBC / healthcare data & AI platform (enabling tech)
**One-liner:** A healthcare data + AI platform ("Healthcare Intelligence Cloud") that unifies
patient data to power population health and value-based care - the private data-infrastructure
play behind VBC.
**Profile last updated:** 2026-06-14

## Business model / how it makes money
SaaS / platform subscriptions. Innovaccer aggregates and normalizes patient data across EHRs,
payers, and care settings into a unified record, then layers analytics, population-health, and
increasingly **AI-native** applications on top - sold to health systems, payers, and VBC
organizations. Revenue is recurring software/ARR, not risk-bearing - it is the data plumbing
that providers and risk-bearers run their VBC programs on (closest public analog: Innovaccer
is to data infrastructure what Tempus's Insights is to genomic data, but for clinical/claims).

## Valuation snapshot
- **Private; ~$3.45B-$3.5B (Jan 2025).** Implied ~14x ARR (on ~$252M est.) - a software-style
  multiple, well above care-delivery VBC names, reflecting recurring-revenue economics. The
  modest step-up from $3.2B (2021) to ~$3.5B (2025) signals a tougher health-tech funding
  environment / flattish valuation, despite ARR growth.

## Why it matters for HC coverage
Innovaccer is the private data/AI infrastructure layer of value-based care and the clearest
read on health-tech software valuations (software multiple on ARR vs. care-delivery EBITDA
multiples). A logical IPO candidate and strategic target, and the comp to reach for when a
VBC conversation turns to the data/enabling-tech stack rather than risk-bearing.
