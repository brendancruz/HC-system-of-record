# Abbott / Exact Sciences

**Date:** Announced 2025-11-20; closed 2026-03-23
**Type:** Strategic M&A (public take-out)
**Acquirer:** Abbott Laboratories (ABT)
**Target:** Exact Sciences (EXAS) - diagnostics, cancer screening + precision oncology
**Sub-sector:** Diagnostics

## Terms
- **$105.00 per share, all-cash.**
- Total equity value **~$21B**.
- Abbott funded partly via a ~$20B debt offering (reported).

## Rationale
Abbott buys the leading non-invasive colorectal-cancer screening franchise (Cologuard /
Cologuard Plus) plus precision oncology (Oncotype DX) and a blood-based pipeline
(Cancerguard MCED, Oncodetect MRD). Adds a large, recurring, reimbursed screening
revenue stream to Abbott's diagnostics segment.

## Valuation read
- Implied **~6.5x FY2025 revenue** ($3.25B) and ~52x FY2025 adj. EBITDA ($400M).
- The benchmark for what a strategic pays for a **scaled, profitable, recurring** dx
  franchise - notably *below* the double-digit sales multiples on pre-profit growth peers
  (GH ~18x, NTRA ~13x). Profitability + recurring volume, not just growth, is what
  cleared the deal.

## Coverage angle
The marquee diagnostics deal of the cycle. Reframes dx M&A: large-cap medtech will pay up
for cash-generative screening at ~6-7x sales, which sets a floor/anchor for valuing the
rest of the screening field and validates the strategic scarcity of reimbursed franchises.
